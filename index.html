About Me

As a small child I was always fiddling with something. Small pieces of wood, plastic, and metal always held my attention because I could see future possibilities for each item. Eventually, imagining those possibilities led me to take wood shop in High school. A teacher noticed that my hands were very steady when creating detail work or using the class machines. He suggested I look at some of the technical courses offered by the school district. I took an electrical class, really enjoyed it, and started seeing lots of possibilities for my future. I decided to start by obtaining IPC-620 and J-Standard certification. I thought of starting the journey to be an electrician but when my wife & I needed a computer that could hold lots of storage and I couldn't find anything to fit our needs I decided to try building one. It was incredibly fun. Suddenly, wondered if I can build this what else can I learn about computers?
